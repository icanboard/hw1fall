How does a person fish for fun?

Always bring something to drink. When you are standing near a body of water, you will eventaully get thirsty and drinking from the water you are fishing in is not recommended.

Other than water, here are some things you should consider taking:

  1. snacks
  2. bait
  3. pole
  4. tackle
  5. something to sit on

A List of Pros and Cons for Fishing

Pros
  • Catching your own dinner is rewarding.
  • Fishing is relaxing.
  • You can catch trophy fish and brag about them to your friends.
Cons
  • It stinks when you don't catch fish.
  • Getting tangles in your line can be stressful.
  • No one knows if you are telling the truth about your stories without proof.
